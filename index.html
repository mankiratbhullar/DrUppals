Our Services

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a specially of dentistry that deals with the diagnosis ,prevention and correction of malpositioned teeth and jaws.

Implants

Dental implants replace missing teeth and what to expect during treatment.Find the types of dental implants and procedure options.Plan for your brighter smile now.

Kids Dentistry

All types of dental treatment done in children ranging from preventive procedures(like diet counselling,oral hygiene demonstration,fluoride application and pit and fissure sealants) to intervention procedures like root canal therapy,space maintainers,habit breaking appliances in children.We educate and empower the patients to have a positive attitude towards oral health care.

Endodontic/ Root Canal Treatment

Root canal is a treatment to repair and save a badly damaged or infected tooth instead of removing it.The procedure involves removing the damaged area of tooth(the pulp) and cleaning and disinfecting it,then filling and sealing it.

Sports Dentistry

Sports Dentistry(SD) acts in the prevention ,maintenance and treatment of oral and facial injuries as well as the collection and dissemination of information on dental trauma,beyond stimulus to research.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr.Uppal's improve your smile by cosmetic dentistry.Cosmetic dentistry is a method of professional oral care that focuses on improving the appearance of your mouth,teeth and smile.

Preventive Dental Care

Dr.Uppal's will help to get healthy teeth with preventive dental care by avoiding cavities,gum disease,enamel wear and more.

Invisalign

Invisalign transparent braces help you have a better smile.Get a better smile to shine through invisalign successfully.

Crowns & Bridges

Both crowns and most bridges are fixed prosthetic devices.Unlike removable devices such as dentures,which you can take out and clean daily,crowns and bridges are cemented onto existing teeth or implants,and can only be removed by a dentist
